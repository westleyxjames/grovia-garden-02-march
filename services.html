Our Professional Gardening Services

Comprehensive gardening and landscape maintenance services tailored for residential properties in Davie, Florida. From routine lawn care to specialized plant maintenance, we provide reliable service to keep your outdoor spaces healthy and attractive year-round.

Lawn Care & Maintenance

Keep your lawn healthy, green, and well-manicured with our comprehensive lawn care services. We provide routine maintenance tailored to South Florida's growing season and grass types, ensuring your lawn looks its best throughout the year.

Our lawn care services adapt to seasonal needs, with adjusted mowing heights for different times of year. We understand Florida's unique climate challenges including heat stress, heavy rainfall periods, and rapid growth cycles.

What's Included:

  • Weekly or bi-weekly mowing based on growth rate
  • Professional edging along walkways, driveways, and garden beds
  • Grass height management for optimal health and appearance
  • Clipping removal and debris cleanup after each service
  • Seasonal adjustments for Florida's weather patterns
  • String trimming around obstacles and hard-to-reach areas

Best For: Homeowners seeking consistent, reliable lawn maintenance without the hassle of equipment ownership or time commitment.

Garden Planting & Design

Transform your outdoor space with thoughtfully selected plants suited to Florida's climate. Our garden design and planting services focus on creating sustainable, attractive landscapes that thrive in local conditions.

We work with native and adapted plant species that require less water and maintenance while providing year-round color and interest. Our selections consider sun exposure, soil conditions, and your property's specific microclimate.

Service Features:

  • Climate-appropriate plant selection for South Florida
  • Native and drought-tolerant species recommendations
  • Garden bed preparation with proper soil amendment
  • Professional planting with proper spacing and depth
  • Mulching for moisture retention and weed suppression
  • Color coordination and seasonal planting options
  • Initial care instructions for new plantings

Popular Choices: Hibiscus, ixora, crotons, bromeliads, and various palm species that thrive in Broward County's climate.

Hedge & Shrub Trimming

Maintain the shape, health, and appearance of your hedges, shrubs, and ornamental plants with our professional trimming services. Regular pruning promotes healthy growth, improves plant structure, and keeps your landscape looking neat and well-maintained.

Our team understands proper pruning techniques for different plant species, ensuring cuts are made at the right time and location to encourage healthy regrowth without causing stress or damage to your plants.

Our Approach:

  • Shape maintenance for formal hedges and privacy screens
  • Health-focused pruning to remove dead or diseased branches
  • Size control to prevent overgrowth near structures
  • Flowering shrub trimming timed for optimal blooms
  • Complete debris removal and cleanup
  • Growth management for long-term plant health

Timing Matters: We schedule trimming based on plant type and growth patterns to maximize health and appearance.

Seasonal Yard Cleanup

Prepare your property for changing seasons and recover from storm damage with our comprehensive yard cleanup services. We remove debris, clear overgrowth, and restore your outdoor space to a clean, well-maintained condition.

Florida's weather can create significant yard maintenance challenges from falling palm fronds to post-storm debris. Our cleanup services address these issues efficiently, helping your property recover quickly and look its best.

Cleanup Services:

  • Complete leaf and debris removal from lawn and beds
  • Post-storm cleanup including branch and palm frond removal
  • Seasonal transition preparation for wet and dry seasons
  • Overgrowth clearing in neglected areas
  • General property tidying and organization
  • Hauling and proper disposal of yard waste

Hurricane Season: We offer priority storm cleanup services during Florida's hurricane season (June-November).

Irrigation System Checkups

Ensure your irrigation system operates efficiently with our basic inspection and maintenance services. We identify common issues that waste water, create dry spots, or lead to overwatering, helping you maintain a healthy landscape while conserving water.

Proper irrigation is crucial in Florida's climate, especially during dry season. Our checkups help catch problems early before they lead to dead zones in your lawn or unnecessarily high water bills.

Inspection Includes:

  • Visual inspection of sprinkler heads and zones
  • Leak identification at connections and valves
  • Coverage assessment to identify dry or over-watered areas
  • Sprinkler head adjustment for proper coverage
  • Basic maintenance recommendations
  • Timer and controller functionality check

Note: We provide basic inspections and maintenance. Complex repairs or system installations may require a licensed irrigation specialist.

Comprehensive Property Maintenance

Keep your entire outdoor space well-maintained with our comprehensive property care services. We handle ongoing upkeep tasks that preserve your landscape's appearance and prevent small issues from becoming larger problems.

This service is ideal for homeowners who want a single, reliable provider handling all outdoor maintenance tasks. We create a customized maintenance plan based on your property's specific needs and your preferences.

Maintenance Tasks:

  • Walkway and pathway maintenance and edging
  • Garden bed weeding and mulch maintenance
  • Weed management in lawn and landscaped areas
  • Deadheading and general plant grooming
  • Overall property tidying and appearance maintenance
  • Monitoring for pest or disease issues
  • Seasonal adjustments based on weather and growth

Flexible Plans: We create maintenance schedules ranging from weekly to monthly based on your property's needs.

Tailored to Florida's Climate

South Florida's subtropical climate presents unique challenges and opportunities for gardening and landscaping. With warm temperatures year-round, high humidity, distinct wet and dry seasons, and occasional severe weather events, maintaining attractive outdoor spaces requires local knowledge and experience.

Our services are designed specifically for Davie and the surrounding Broward County area. We understand which plants thrive in our sandy soil, how to manage growth during rainy season, and when to adjust maintenance during drier periods. From heat-tolerant plant selections to seasonal maintenance schedules that account for Florida's weather patterns, we provide care that works with the local environment rather than against it.
